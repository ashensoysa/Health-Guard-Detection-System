📖 Disease Guide for Doctors

🩺 Hypertension

Trigger: Systolic > 140 or Diastolic > 90

Care Tips: Low-sodium diet, cardio exercise, avoid stress

🫁 Hypoxemia

Trigger: SpO₂ < 95%

Care Tips: Use humidifiers, breathing exercises, oxygen therapy

❤️ Tachycardia

Trigger: Heart Rate > 100 bpm

Care Tips: Hydration, rest, reduce caffeine, evaluate medications

📉 Hypotension

Trigger: Systolic < 90 or Diastolic < 60

Care Tips: Increase fluid/salt intake, monitor posture, avoid heavy lifting
